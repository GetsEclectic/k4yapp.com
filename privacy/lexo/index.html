Lexo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-15

This Privacy Policy applies to Lexo (com.lexo.keyboard), published by K4Y Apps. Lexo is a privacy-first keyboard for Android.

Summary: No data leaves your device

Lexo is a fully local keyboard. Everything it does — text prediction, autocorrect, personalization, and all on-device ML inference — runs entirely on your device and stays there. No keystrokes, typed text, or any other data ever leaves your device. There is no account, no cloud sync, no analytics, no telemetry, and no advertising of any kind.

Data the app collects

None. Lexo does not transmit any data off your device. It does not use any analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising SDKs.

Data the app shares with third parties

None. Lexo shares no data with any third party. There are no third-party SDKs included in the app.

Third-party SDKs in use

None. Lexo contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or telemetry SDKs.

Your choices

  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data (keyboard personalization and learned words) from your device.

Children's privacy

Lexo is not directed at children under 13. Because Lexo collects no data, there is nothing to delete, but if you have questions please contact us at hello@k4yapp.com.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@k4yapp.com.
